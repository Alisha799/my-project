About EmTeK CoE

EmTeK CoE is dedicated to building a robust ecosystem in Industry 4.0, AI, Cyber Security, and Analytics. It empowers startups, entrepreneurs, and industries by providing mentorship, infrastructure, and innovation opportunities.

Mission

Accelerate indigenous innovation and entrepreneurship in Electronics, EVs, Embedded Systems, and IoT.

Vision

To be a premier national hub for Industry 4.0 excellence, fostering scalable and globally competitive startups.

Objectives

  • Support 150+ startups in Industry 4.0
  • Enable technology commercialization
  • Bridge academia, industry & R&D
  • Facilitate funding & global exposure
