About Us

EmTek CoE focuses at creating a high potential ecosystem for carrying out Industry 4.0 & Analytics, Cyber Security & AI and other emerging technologies for industrial sector and Startups. It helps in building the skilled manpower and creating many entrepreneurs/Startups in IT sector. The main objective of EmTek CoE is to develop a world class “Industry 4.0 Lab” in Odisha and be the preferred start-up destination of the country. EmTek promotes entrepreneurship and innovation in emerging technology. EmTek brings in a pool of mentors, academicians, investors & consultants in market intelligence with the major focus on Product Development, Technology Enhancement by the application of Industry4.0 solutions.
